Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly:

Email: Send details to cfreylob@gmail.com.
Include affected version(s), steps to reproduce, and any relevant code or screenshots.

Acknowledgment: You will receive an acknowledgment of your report within 48 hours.

Resolution Timeline: Security fixes will be prioritized according to severity. Minor issues may take up to 30 days; critical issues will be addressed immediately.

Disclosure Policy:

Accepted vulnerabilities will be publicly disclosed once a patch or fix is released.

If your report is not considered a vulnerability, you will be notified with an explanation.

Confidentiality: Do not publicly disclose the issue until a fix has been released to avoid putting users at risk.
